Roughly USD 15 billion is spent in India annually (2023) on social sector. Close to 1.9 lac (2023) Indian NGOs endeavor to raise a part of this fund from various sources. These numbers are growing steadily irrespective of many world volatilities. Irrespective of this historical growth trend, the fundraising ecosystem has not evolved much in India. Issues with finding qualified fundraisers, in addition to several other challenges around the capacity of the NGOs to tap this growing social sector funding market have remained endemic problems for several decades now.

BIG is in a good space to solve innumerable highly complex and interesting fundraising problems. We possibly exist in the most diverse and dramatic fundraising dancefloor in the world. Solving some of these fundraising problems may lead to significant human progress, driven by the charities.

Who is BIG

BIG has over one hundred years of collective nonprofit fundraising experience in- frontline sales, strategy development, commanding large fundraising operations, dozens of high value acquisitions, and priceless education from uncountable days and nights of failures and distress. We take pride in our own integrity standards and possess and an unwavering resilient attitude to solve problems. Please look at our upcoming consulting partner page.

Vision

Charities in India will not lose sleep over fundraising problems.

Mission

Become the fundraising engine for fifty charities in India in the next 5 years. Provide access to reliable and valuable fundraising data to the sector. Produce fifty high value fundraisers for the sector in the next 5 years.
